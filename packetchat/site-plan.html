Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of this website is to showcase the previous work that PacketChat has done for hacker conference leaders and local leaders, and to collect leads from interested people who want PacketChat's products.

Audience

The primary audience of this website is leaders of hacker conferences, such as DefCon, SaintCon, and BSides SF, especially those who attend DefCon regularly.
